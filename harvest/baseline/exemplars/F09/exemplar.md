This spring, the 62nd home entered the Harbor Bend Community Land Trust.

Sixty-two is not a round number. We didn't plan a celebration around it. But every home that joins the trust joins it permanently, and that's worth pausing on. The land stays ours forever; the home belongs to the family living in it. When they sell someday, the resale formula keeps the price within reach of the next buyer, and the one after that.

We've watched this work 19 times now. Nineteen resales since 2014, and every single home stayed affordable for the family who came next.

The portfolio behind home 62 goes back to 2011: 41 homes acquired and rehabilitated, 21 built new. Each one took a seller willing to talk to us, neighbors who showed up, funders who stayed patient.

The 63rd is somewhere in Harbor Bend right now. We're looking for it.
